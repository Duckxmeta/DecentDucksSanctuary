Our Story.

It all began with a single lucky duck named Jasmine. What started as an individual act of rescue quickly grew into a vision for something much larger: a dedicated sanctuary where every bird could live a life free of stress and full of care.

We realized that domestic ducks often fall through the cracks of traditional rescue systems. Decent Ducks was born from the idea that community and passion could bridge that gap.

Today, we are pushing this project from a shared dream into a physical reality. Your support isn't just a donation; it's a brick in the foundation of a permanent home for our flock. We are building a place where "Decent" means the highest quality of life possible.
