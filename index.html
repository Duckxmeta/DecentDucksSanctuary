The Decent Ducks Journey

More Than Just Ducks.

It started with a single lucky duck named Jasmine. She was the first life we gave a second chance, becoming the icon of our TikTok and YouTube community and the catalyst for a much bigger dream.

What began as one rescue has transformed into a mission to build permanent, high-quality homes for animals that have been neglected or abandoned. We prioritize stress-free environments where every soul—feathered or fur—has the space to truly chill and thrive.

"Every creature deserves a safe spot to chill & thrive."

The Original Pack

Jade

The loyal heart of our pack. Jade was left behind by a local visitor and found her true family at the sanctuary.

River

River was bought to be a "truck dog," but preferred a peaceful life in the sanctuary instead. He's been here ever since.

Sweet Pea

After her owner passed away, we grew an immediate attachment to Sweet Pea and knew she belonged here for good.

The Gentle Giant

Otis

A retired show horse put out to pasture. When we bought the property, Otis came with it. After a year of patience, he finally trusts us enough to ask for snacks—though he still keeps a bit of his wild spirit.

Permanent Resident

Life at the sanctuary moves fast. We recently welcomed some unexpected news—new puppies may be joining the family soon! River has since been fixed, and both Echo and Cassidy are scheduled for the same as soon as it's safe for them to do so. We are committed to responsible care for every life here.
